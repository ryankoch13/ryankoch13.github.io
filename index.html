About Me.

Hi, my name is Ryan Koch, and I am a full-stack web developer.

For me, that means I get to do some of my favorite things in the world, nerd out about technology and solve logical problems. I chose the field of coding and computer science more generally because I’ve always had a deep love for problem solving and creative expression. Before I found development, this manifested as my stint playing video games competitively, or in music production, or even in my past jobs as a quality manager trying to create new processes to improve efficiency.

I remember listening to a couple of podcasts that really pushed me in the direction of software and technology - Tim Ferris interviewing Naval Ravikant and Lex Fridman interviewing Guido Van Rossum. They helped me understand that computer science is this beautiful field of science that meets at the convergence of all the things that make me curious, chemistry, math, physics, and even biology and consciousness. This amazing place where I could contribute real value to society and be a part of a solution to serious problems that are on the horizon for humanity, whether that be climate change, AI, finance, whatever.

Recently, I’ve spent the last several months mastering ReactJS and RubyOnRails. I would love the opportunity to work together and build something valuable.
